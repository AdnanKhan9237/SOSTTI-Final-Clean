Accreditation with NAVTTC

SOS Technical Training Institute (SOSTTI) is accredited by NAVTTC (National Vocational and Technical Training Commission), ensuring that our programs meet the highest national standards in technical and vocational education. With an overall institute accreditation score of 92.5% (Grade A), our courses in CIT, Automobile Mechanics, HVACR, Welding, Machinist, and Motorcycle Mechanics are recognized for their quality and industry relevance. This accreditation reflects our commitment to providing skill-based, career-focused education that empowers students for the modern workforce.

Registration with NAVTTC

SOS Technical Training Institute (SOSTTI) is registered with NAVTTC (National Vocational and Technical Training Commission), Government of Pakistan. This registration certifies that our institute meets the legal national standards for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET). Being a registered NAVTTC institution ensures that our students receive recognized qualifications, opening doors to better employment opportunities both nationally and internationally.

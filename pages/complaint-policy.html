Complaint Policy

Fair, transparent, and confidential resolution of complaints

Policy Statement

SOS Technical Training Institute is committed to providing a positive and respectful environment for all students, staff, and stakeholders. This complaint policy ensures that any concerns or complaints are addressed promptly, fairly, and confidentially, with the aim of resolving issues effectively and maintaining trust in the institution.

Principles of Complaint Handling

  • Accessibility: Easy access to complaint procedures
  • Fairness: Impartial and unbiased investigation
  • Confidentiality: Protection of complainant's identity
  • Timeliness: Prompt resolution of complaints
  • Transparency: Clear communication throughout the process
  • No Retaliation: Protection against victimization

Types of Complaints

Category Examples Handling Authority
Academic Grading disputes, course content, teaching quality Academic Coordinator
Administrative Admission process, fee issues, documentation Administrative Officer
Facilities Equipment maintenance, classroom conditions Facility Manager
Staff Conduct Professional behavior, discrimination, harassment Human Resources
Student Conduct Bullying, misconduct, disciplinary issues Disciplinary Committee

Complaint Submission Process

Step 1: Informal Resolution

  • Discuss concern directly with relevant person
  • Seek resolution through dialogue
  • Document the discussion and outcome

Step 2: Formal Written Complaint

  • Submit complaint form available at office or website
  • Provide detailed description with supporting evidence
  • Include desired resolution
  • Submit to relevant authority based on complaint type

Step 3: Investigation

  • Acknowledgment within 3 working days
  • Thorough and impartial investigation
  • Interviews with relevant parties
  • Collection of evidence and documentation

Step 4: Resolution

  • Decision communicated within 15 working days
  • Clear explanation of findings
  • Implementation of corrective actions
  • Follow-up to ensure resolution

Confidentiality & Protection

Confidentiality Assurance: All complaints are handled with strict confidentiality. The identity of complainants is protected, and no retaliation against complainants will be tolerated.

  • Access to complaint information restricted to authorized personnel
  • Anonymous complaints accepted but may limit investigation
  • Complainant's identity not disclosed without consent
  • Strict action against false or malicious complaints
  • Protection from victimization guaranteed

Appeals Process

  • Appeal available if dissatisfied with initial resolution
  • Appeal must be submitted within 10 working days
  • Appeal reviewed by higher authority or committee
  • Final decision communicated within 10 working days
  • External mediation available for unresolved issues

Timeframes

Stage Maximum Timeframe
Acknowledgment 3 working days
Initial Resolution 15 working days
Appeal Decision 10 working days
Complex Cases 30 working days (with updates)

Record Keeping

  • All complaints recorded in centralized database
  • Records maintained for minimum 3 years
  • Statistical analysis for trend identification
  • Annual reporting to management committee
  • Continuous improvement based on feedback

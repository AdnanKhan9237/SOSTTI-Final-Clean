Examination Policy

Fair, transparent, and standardized assessment procedures

Policy Statement

SOS Technical Training Institute is committed to maintaining high standards of academic integrity and fair assessment. Our examination policy ensures that all students are evaluated consistently and transparently based on their knowledge, skills, and competencies.

Examination Schedule

  • Mid-term examinations are conducted after 50% of course completion
  • Final examinations are conducted at the end of each course
  • Practical examinations are scheduled alongside theoretical exams
  • Examination timetable is displayed two weeks before exams
  • No changes to schedule once published

Examination Rules & Regulations

Before Examination:

  • Students must carry their institute ID card
  • Arrive at examination hall 15 minutes before start time
  • Only permitted materials allowed in examination hall
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices strictly prohibited

During Examination:

  • Strict silence must be maintained
  • No communication between students
  • Raise hand for any queries or assistance
  • No leaving examination hall without permission

Assessment Criteria

Component Weightage Description
Theoretical Exams 20% Written tests covering course content
Practical Exams 80% Hands-on skills assessment

Grading System

Marks Range Grade Remarks
80% and above A+ Outstanding
70% - 79% A Excellent
60% - 69% B Good
50% - 59% C Satisfactory
40% - 49% D Pass
Below 40% F Fail

Malpractice & Disciplinary Action

Zero Tolerance Policy: Any form of examination malpractice will result in strict disciplinary action.

  • Copying or attempting to copy - Immediate disqualification
  • Possession of unauthorized materials - Cancellation of paper
  • Impersonation - Expulsion from institute
  • Disruptive behavior - Suspension from examinations

Re-examination & Improvement

  • Supplementary exams available for failed subjects
  • Re-examination fee applies
  • Maximum two attempts allowed per subject
  • Grade improvement exams available for passed subjects
  • Best grade considered for final certification

Result Declaration & Certification

  • Results declared within 15 working days after exams
  • Mark sheets available for collection from office
  • Certificates awarded upon successful completion
  • Duplicate certificates available with applicable fee and application
  • Transcripts provided for further education or employment

Policy Review

This examination policy is reviewed annually by the Academic Committee to ensure it remains relevant and effective.

Aims & Objectives

Our commitment to excellence in technical education and community empowerment

Our Primary Aim

SOS Technical Training Institute (INFAQ Foundation Campus) aims at providing a high standard of technical and vocational training to SOS children, students of Korangi Academy and other under privileged and deserving boys and girls as well as the community at large to meet the entry level needs of the job market in their chosen technical field.

After acquiring technical education, students will be in a position to secure gainful employment to support their families and live in dignity to become good citizens of Pakistan. Also by joining the pool of skilled manpower the students will be contributing to the economic progress and prosperity of Pakistan.

Our Core Objectives

Quality Technical Programs

Offer comprehensive and industry-relevant technical programs that meet national and international standards, ensuring our graduates are equipped with the skills demanded by today's job market.

Experienced Instructors

Provide instructors who have extensive industry experience and practical knowledge, bringing real-world insights into the classroom and workshop environments.

Balanced Education

Provide both theoretical knowledge and hands-on applications, creating well-rounded professionals who understand the principles behind their practical skills.

Real Workplace Simulation

Provide training in workshops that simulate actual workplace environments, preparing students for the challenges and expectations of their future careers.

Modern Equipment

Provide up-to-date tools and equipment for students to practice with, ensuring they are familiar with the latest technologies and methodologies in their fields.

Industry Collaboration

Work closely with business and industry to establish and update curricula, maintaining alignment with current market needs and emerging trends.

Professional Development

Provide educational training in work readiness skills and work ethics to inculcate good work habits and attitudes, preparing students for professional success.

Internship Opportunities

Assist students in finding on-the-job internships to help with transition from institute to workplace on completion of the courses, bridging the gap between education and employment.

Job Placement Support

Provide students with job search skills and assistance with job placements, ensuring our graduates successfully enter the workforce and begin their careers.

Admission Information

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Middle (for most courses)
  • Age: 14-35 years
  • Basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Passing entry test/interview
  • No discrimination on basis of gender, religion, or ethnicity

Admission Process

  1. Application Submission: Complete application form with required documents
  2. Entry Test: Basic subject knowledge
  3. Interview: Personal interview with Instructor
  4. Document Verification: Submit copy of documents for verification
  5. Fee Submission: Payment of course fees
  6. Registration: Final registration and course allocation

Required Documents

  • Matriculation/Middle certificate
  • CNIC/B-Form copy
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Father's/Guardian's CNIC copy

Admission Schedule

  • Spring Session: Admissions open in May/June
  • Fall Session: Admissions open in November/December
  • Early applications encouraged
  • Limited seats available

Automobile Mechanic / Automobile Electrician Course

6 Months CBT Level-II, III, IV NAVTTC Certified Multiple Batches

Course Overview

The Automobile Mechanic / Automobile Electrician course is designed to develop the practical skills and technical knowledge required to maintain, service, and repair modern vehicles. This program provides hands-on experience and theoretical understanding of automotive systems, preparing trainees for employment in the automotive industry or self-employment in vehicle maintenance workshops.

Course Curriculum for Level-II

Our comprehensive curriculum is divided into 9 modules

Demonstrate Communication Skills

Maintain Safe Work Environment

Repair Ignition System

Repair Fuel System (Carburetor)

Repair Engine Cooling System

Repair Engine Lubrication System

Repair Braking Systems

Carry out Wheel Balancing and Alignment

Occupational Health and Safety

Career Opportunities for Automobile Mechanic

Graduates of our Automobile Mechanic course can pursue a wide range of opportunities in the automotive service and maintenance sector:

  • Automobile Mechanic
  • Workshop Technician
  • Engine Overhaul Mechanic
  • Vehicle Maintenance Supervisor
  • Service Advisor
  • Fleet Maintenance Technician
  • Motor Vehicle Inspector
  • Self-Employed Auto Mechanic / Workshop Owner

  • Auto Diagnostic Technician
  • Vehicle Wiring Technician

Course Overview

The Artificial Intelligence (AI) course is designed to introduce students to the fascinating world of intelligent systems and machine learning. This comprehensive program covers the fundamentals of AI, including its history, types, and real-world applications. Students will also gain hands-on experience with prompt engineering, AI tools, and Python programming, enabling them to create, train, and optimize intelligent models. By the end of the course, students will be equipped with the skills needed to pursue careers in AI development, data science, and automation engineering.

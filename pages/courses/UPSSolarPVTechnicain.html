Course Overview

The UPS & Solar PV Technician course is designed to provide students with the technical knowledge and practical skills required to work with uninterrupted power supply systems and solar energy installations. This 3-months program covers system design, installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of both UPS and solar PV systems, preparing students for careers in the growing renewable energy and power management industry.

Course Overview

The Mechanical Turner Course provides comprehensive training in precision machining using conventional lathes and related machine tools. Students learn to read technical drawings, measure tolerances, set up workpieces, and produce accurate components for industrial and mechanical applications. The course also emphasizes safety practices, machine maintenance, and the use of measuring instruments to ensure high-quality production.

Course Overview

The Mobile Phone Repairing Course provides hands-on training in diagnosing and repairing hardware and software issues in smartphones and tablets. Students learn about mobile circuitry, component identification, soldering, display replacement, and software flashing. The course equips learners with the skills to work in repair shops, service centers, or start their own mobile repair business with confidence.

Course Overview

The Graphic Designing course is designed to equip students with creative and technical skills needed in the modern digital world. This 6-month program covers everything from basic design principles to advanced tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator enabling students to create professional visuals for print and digital media. By the end of the course, students will be prepared for roles such as graphic designer, visual artist, and digital content creator in various creative industries.

Course Overview

The General & Industrial Electrician Course offers comprehensive training in electrical wiring,power distribution, and industrial control systems. Students learn to interpret electrical diagrams, install and maintain electrical equipment, and apply safety standards for both domestic and industrial environments. The course also covers troubleshooting, motor control circuits, and modern electrical practices aligned with industry requirements.

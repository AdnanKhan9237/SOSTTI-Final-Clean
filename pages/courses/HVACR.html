Course Overview

The Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning, and Refrigeration (HVACR) course provides comprehensive training in system design, installation, troubleshooting, and preventive maintenance. Students learn to handle refrigerants safely, understand electrical components, and apply modern diagnostic techniques. This program prepares learners for careers as HVACR technicians, maintenance professionals, or entrepreneurs in the growing cooling and climate control industry.

Attendance Policy

Maintaining consistent attendance for academic success and professional development

Policy Statement

SOS Technical Training Institute recognizes that regular attendance is essential for academic success and skill development. This attendance policy establishes clear expectations for both students and staff, ensuring consistent participation in educational activities while accommodating genuine needs for absence.

Student Attendance Requirements

Minimum Attendance Requirements

  • 75% minimum attendance required for all courses
  • Attendance calculated based on total scheduled classes
  • Both theoretical and practical sessions count toward attendance
  • Special consideration for medical emergencies with documentation
Attendance Range Status Consequences
90% and above Excellent Eligible for merit certificates and awards
75% - 89% Satisfactory Eligible for examinations
65% - 74% Warning Written warning and parent/guardian notification
Below 65% Critical May be barred from final examinations

Attendance Recording System

  • Biometric attendance system for accurate recording
  • Manual attendance register as backup
  • Daily attendance reports generated
  • Monthly attendance summaries shared with students
  • Parents/guardians notified when attendance drops below 70%

Late Arrival Policy

Punctuality Requirement: Students are expected to arrive on time for all classes. Late arrivals disrupt learning and show disrespect to instructors and fellow students.

  • Grace period of 5 minutes allowed for unavoidable delays
  • Arrival after 15 minutes marked as half absence
  • Three late arrivals equal one full absence
  • Persistent lateness may lead to disciplinary action
  • Valid reasons for lateness must be documented

Leave of Absence Procedures

Planned Absences

  • Advance written application required
  • Maximum 5 consecutive days for personal reasons
  • Supporting documents for extended leaves
  • Approval from course coordinator necessary

Emergency Absences

  • Inform institute within 24 hours of absence
  • Submit supporting documents upon return
  • Medical certificates required for sick leave exceeding 3 days
  • Make-up classes arranged where possible

Staff Attendance Requirements

Staff Category Working Hours Attendance Method
Teaching Staff 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM Biometric + class attendance records
Administrative Staff 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM Biometric system
Technical Staff As per workshop schedule Manual register + supervisor verification

Monitoring & Reporting

  • Weekly attendance reports reviewed by department heads
  • Monthly attendance analysis presented to management
  • Early warning system for at-risk attendance patterns
  • Counseling sessions for students with poor attendance
  • Performance reviews include attendance evaluation

Consequences of Poor Attendance

For Students:

  • Written warning after 3 consecutive absences
  • Parent/guardian meeting when attendance drops below 70%
  • Suspension from practical sessions if attendance inadequate
  • Barred from examinations if below minimum requirement
  • Course repetition required for excessive absences

For Staff:

  • Verbal warning for pattern of unauthorized absences
  • Written warning for repeated violations
  • Impact on performance appraisal and increments
  • Disciplinary action for habitual absenteeism
  • Termination for gross violation of attendance policy

Appeals & Exceptional Circumstances

  • Appeals committee for attendance-related disputes
  • Medical emergencies considered with proper documentation
  • Family emergencies accommodated with verification
  • Special consideration for students with disabilities
  • Case-by-case evaluation for unique circumstances

Policy Review

This attendance policy is reviewed annually by the Academic Committee to ensure it remains effective in promoting consistent attendance while being fair and reasonable to all stakeholders.
